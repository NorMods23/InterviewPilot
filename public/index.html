About Us: The Studgram Advantage

We believe preparation is the key to success. Our platform uses advanced AI (Large Language Models) to simulate a high-pressure interview environment, focusing on real-time speech-to-text, tone analysis, and deep technical evaluation based on your resume and branch of study.

Key Features

  • Resume-Aware Questioning: AI frames questions based on your uploaded resume and stated skills.
  • Adaptive Difficulty: Choose your level (Beginner to Expert) to control question complexity.
  • Voice Analysis: Get feedback on your pace, tone, and confidence level.
  • Detailed Report: A comprehensive, downloadable report covering strengths, weaknesses, and a selection percentage.

How We Outshine Competitors

Unlike basic mock interview tools, we utilize a full-stack architecture that incorporates advanced PDF parsing, personalized question branching (mixing general, skill-specific, and requested topics), and an integrated voice interface, providing a truly immersive and relevant experience.
